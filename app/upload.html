What this is — and isn't

  • This is a calibrated probabilistic estimate, not a verdict. Facial expression maps to emotion with substantial variance (Barrett 2019).
  • Fast CNN: EfficientNet-B4 trained in-house on AffectNet 6-class (anger, disgust, fear, happiness, sadness, surprise), face-cropped + aligned — held-out test accuracy 72.3% (TTA). Cannot predict neutral / contempt.
  • Calibrated FACS: py-feat (RetinaFace + MobileFaceNet + XGB AUs + ResMaskNet) — 7 emotions + 20 Action Units. Slower but gives the AU breakdown.
  • No lie / intent / deception inference — the science doesn't support it (DePaulo 2003).
