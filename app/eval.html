How this works

For each emotion you get a 3s prep cue, then a 2s capture window. The model's most frequent prediction in that window is the prediction for that trial. Accuracy = correct / total; the confusion matrix shows where it confuses one emotion for another (fear↔surprise is the classic one).

This measures the emotion-prototype layer (AU constellations) under deliberate posing — a controlled baseline, not in-the-wild accuracy. Extend later with recorded labeled clips.
