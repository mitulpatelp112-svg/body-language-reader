What it reads

Five signals, read together.

Any one cue is weak and easy to misread. Presence fuses five independent channels, so a read only shows when they agree.

Face, in full detail

A 478-point mesh tracks the facial action units behind real expressions, then maps them to the seven core emotions as constellations, not single muscles.

Posture and gesture

Full-body pose plus both hands: lean, openness, shrugs, and the gestures that carry meaning.

Voice

Pitch, energy, and timbre become dimensional arousal, dominance, and valence while you speak.

Pulse

Your heart rate, read contactlessly from tiny color shifts in your skin. Harder to fake than a posed face.

The synthesis

Signals vote into evidence-backed reads: engagement, rapport, confidence, discomfort.

Measure. Corroborate. Coach.

The pipeline is built to earn confidence, not manufacture it.

Measure

On-device, at video rate

Face mesh, pose, hands, voice, and pulse, all computed in your browser. No frames are uploaded.

Corroborate

Many cues must agree

A read only appears when several independent signals point the same way. A lone cue stays honestly tentative.

Coach

One clear, useful tip

You get a plain-language read of how you are coming across, and one thing to adjust.

Most emotion AI overpromises. Presence is built the other way.

Every read carries its confidence and its caveat. The thresholds are grounded in the research the project cites, and the system will never claim to detect lies or hidden intent, because that science does not exist.
