Reads built from constellations

A construct only shows when several independent signals agree. That is the scientifically supported way to read behaviour, and where confidence is earned.

Every signal, rated

Grouped by what does the measuring. The tag is the evidence strength; the number is how much to trust the meaning, out of five.

What it will not do

No lie or intent detection. The research does not support reading deception from nonverbal behaviour (observers land near 55 percent, barely above chance). Facial expressions are also variable across people and cultures. Presence surfaces probabilities with caveats, never verdicts, and everything runs on your device.
